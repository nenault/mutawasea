Mutawasea is a simulation of the human disaster that occurs every day in the Mediterranean Sea. In makeshift boats, people are crammed together, trying to cross the few hundred kilometers that separate them from the shores of the European Union. A few rare boats are trying to help them. In this simulation, you play the captain of one of these boats, you will have to make choices between coming to the rescue of all the boats you encounter or focusing on those that are most in danger. Click where you want to steer your boat, on the open sea or towards the main ports to the north.
If they don't deny you the opportunity to dock there.
